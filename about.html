About FitFlow

Helping students stay consistent with fitness

FitFlow is a personalized fitness coaching platform built for college students who are new to lifting. Instead of forcing a rigid routine, FitFlow uses class schedules, availability, goals, and experience level to create a plan that fits real student life.

What FitFlow Solves

Most students don’t fail because they lack motivation. They fall off when generic plans don’t match changing schedules. FitFlow solves this by making workouts flexible, beginner-friendly, and adaptive when life gets busy.

How It Feels Different

Every plan is personalized and easy to follow. If a workout is missed, FitFlow adjusts the week so users can stay on track without restarting from zero.
